More Than an App. It's Our Community.

I am odo-mate, and just like you, my passion is the open road. I've spent countless hours on my bike and just as many hours wishing for a single app that does it all. So, I decided to build it myself. odo-mate is my answer—a solo project born from a love for riding. This isn't just code; it's a commitment to building the best tool for our community. Join me on this journey.

Everything Your Motorcycle Needs, In Your Pocket.

Smart Ride Tracking

Log every journey with precision. Save your favorite routes and revisit your best rides using Open Street Maps.

Proactive Maintenance

Track past and upcoming services. odo-mate automatically updates your odometer and notifies you when maintenance is due.

Your Digital Garage

Add all your motorcycles in one place. Monitor the status and service history for each bike individually.

Automatic Odometer

Keep your bike's mileage perfectly in sync without manual entry. Focus on the ride, not the paperwork.

Multi-Language Support

odo-mate speaks your language. We're constantly adding more to welcome riders from around the globe.

Route History

Your personal logbook of every adventure. See where you've been and plan where you'll go next.
